Game Mode

  • Press A to move camera to the left
  • Press D to move camera to the right
  • Press E to grab the revolver
  • Press N to switch between day or night
  • Press R to start/stop the rain (only works in level 2 and 3)
  • Click on the left mouse button to shoot

View Mode

  • Press R to activate/desactivate the camera's autorotate mode
  • Press the mouse's left button to rotate the scene
  • Use the scroll's mouse to zoom in/zoom out
  • Press the button bellow the moon to switch between day or night
  • Press the button bellow the cloud to start/stop the rain (only apeears when viewing level 2 and level 3)
